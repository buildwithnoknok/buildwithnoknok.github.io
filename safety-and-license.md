## ⚠️ Safety notice

noknok products are **electronic devices and do-it-yourself maker kits**. You assemble, solder, 3D-print, flash, power, modify, and operate them **at your own risk**. In particular:

- Electronics carry risks of electric shock, short circuits, heat, and fire if mishandled.
- Use only appropriate, correctly-rated power supplies and cables.
- Soldering and 3D printing involve high temperatures — follow proper safety practice.
- Kits contain small parts — **not suitable for young children without adult supervision**; keep away from children under 3.
- Custom firmware, hardware modifications, and third-party builds are **unsupported and entirely at your own risk**.
